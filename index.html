Our Services

Public Dip

Our swimming pool is open to the public on an hourly basis, offering a welcoming and inclusive environment for all. Enjoy a refreshing swim, relax by the poolside, and take advantage of our clean, well-maintained facilities. Whether you're looking for a quick dip or an afternoon of fun, our pool is the perfect place for you and your family

Swimming Classes

Join our 12-day swimming classes designed for all skill levels. Conducted in batches, each session provides comprehensive training and personalized attention from experienced instructors. Each batch lasts for a full 12 days, ensuring consistent progress and skill development. Perfect for beginners and those looking to refine their techniques.

Membership Packages

We offer flexible membership packages for those who enjoy daily swimming. Choose from monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, and annual options to suit your needs. With a membership, enjoy unlimited access during pool hours on working days, allowing you to swim at your convenience. Perfect for regular swimmers seeking value and flexibility.

Advanced Classes

For swimmers who already know the basics or have completed our 12-day course, we offer personalized advanced classes. These sessions focus on teaching new techniques and a variety of swimming strokes. Our expert instructors customize the training to help you refine your skills and reach new levels of proficiency. Ideal for those looking to elevate their swimming capabilities.

Competitive Training

We offer specialized training for children aiming to compete at the highest levels. Our experienced NAS coaches provide expert guidance and rigorous training programs tailored to each child's needs. With a focus on competitive techniques and performance, we help young swimmers achieve their full potential and excel in competitions.
